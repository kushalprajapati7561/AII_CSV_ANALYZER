# Sales Workflow Output Templates

## 1. Meeting Brief Template

```markdown
# Account Brief: {Account Name}

## Background
- Company: {Company Name} | Industry: {Industry} | Size: {Employees/Revenue}
- Relationship: {Duration/Products/Projects}
- Current Stage: {Initial Contact / Evaluation / Negotiation / Active Customer}

## Key Contacts
| Name | Title | Role | Focus |
|------|-------|------|-------|
| {Name} | {Title} | Decision Maker / Influencer / End User | {Focus Area} |

## Meeting Objectives
1. {Primary objective}
2. {Secondary objective}

## Previous Meeting Recap
- Date: {Date}
- Conclusions: {Key conclusions}
- Action Items: {Items and status}

## Recommended Approach
- {Suggested angle}
- {Materials to prepare}
- {Risks and mitigation}
```

## 3. Account Prioritization Table

```markdown
# Account Prioritization

| Priority | Account | Stage | Deal Size | Urgency | Recommended Action |
|:---:|:---|---:|:---:|:---:|---|
| P0 | {Account} | Negotiation | High | High | Schedule executive visit this week |
| P1 | {Account} | Evaluation | Medium | Medium | Prepare custom proposal |
| P2 | {Account} | Initial Contact | Low | Low | Periodic follow-up |
| P3 | {Account} | Dormant | — | Low | Re-engagement email sequence |

## Ranking Criteria
- P0: Likely to close this month — focus resources
- P1: Potential close within 3 months — maintain engagement
- P2: Long-term nurture — periodic touchpoints
- P3: No active opportunity — minimum cadence
```
