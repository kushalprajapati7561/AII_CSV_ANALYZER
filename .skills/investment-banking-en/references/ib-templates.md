## 4. Pitch Deck Outline Template

```markdown
# Pitch Deck: {Project Name}

1. **Cover** — Transaction name, date, team
2. **Executive Summary** — Key highlights and recommendations
3. **Market Overview** — Industry trends, market size, growth drivers
4. **Transaction Capabilities** — Team experience, track record, industry expertise
5. **Case Studies** — 2-3 representative transactions
6. **Target Company Analysis** — Overview, financials, valuation
7. **Proposed Transaction Structure** — Consideration, financing, timeline
8. **Team Introduction** — Key team member backgrounds
9. **Appendix** — Detailed financials, disclaimers
```

## 5. CIM Structure Template

```markdown
# Confidential Information Memorandum: {Company Name}

## Chapter Structure
1. **Executive Summary**
2. **Company Overview** — History, business model, competitive advantages
3. **Industry Analysis** — Market size, growth drivers, competitive landscape
4. **Products & Services** — Product lines, technology, customer base
5. **Sales & Marketing** — Channels, pricing, customer acquisition
6. **Financial Analysis** — Historical financials, KPIs, quality of earnings
7. **Projections** — Revenue forecast, cost structure, EBITDA
8. **Management** — Key team backgrounds
9. **Data Room Index**
10. **Appendix**
```
